About Us

RaftersCo,a white water rafting company, was founded with a purpose to provide exhilarating experiences while promoting safety and evironmental stewardship. Our mission is to create lifelong memories for our customers, while adhering to our creed of "Respect the River,Respect Each Other." Our motto,"Paddle with Passion," reflects our commitment to delivering exceptional service and unforgetable adventures

History

RaftersCo was founded in 1999 by a group of passionate white water rafting enthusiasts. Our mission is to provide exceptional rafting experiences while promoting safety and enviromental stewardship. As the popularity of rafting and adventure sports grew, so did we. We expanded our product line to include kayaking, rock climbing, and camping gear, and opened new locations in Utah, Arizona, and Oregon. We continued to innovate, partnering with leading outdoor brands to introduce new products and technologies. We also collaborated with environmental organizations to promote sustainability and conservation in the outdoor industry.

Today, RaftersCo is a leading outdoor retailer with a strong online presence and multiple locations across the United States. We remain committed to our core values: Excellent customer service, Environmental stewardship. Inspiring people to explore and enjoy the great outdoors.
